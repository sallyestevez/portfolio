About Me

Hi, I'm Sally Estevez. I'm a user experience designer and web developer based in Brooklyn, New York. I study Integrated Design and Media (IDM) at New York University's Tandon School of Engineering and will be graduating in May 2023. I decided to pursue IDM because I was drawn to the creative aspects of technology.

I have been programming since my junior year of high school. An alumni of Code Nation, I have gotten first place at their annual hackathon twice - once in 2018 and once in 2019.

I enjoy learning new skills. I'm always eager to use what I learned in current projects! I hope to create applications that help make people's lives easier by ustilizing my expertise in design and web development.

In my free time I enjoy reading, listening to music, going on walks, knitting, and playing video games.
